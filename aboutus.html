About Us
Helping people find people who needs help

We are the creators of The Help Now App. Us being out of Dallas, TX, it hit close to home when Hurricane Harvey hit the Houston area. We noticed that hundreds of people were in need of desperate help, and hundreds more flocked down to Houston to do just that, help. But, the ones who wanted to help had no way of finding those who needed it. The Help Now team decided to try and do something about that by creating The Help Now App which is designed to help people find those who need help, and help the ones that are in need of assistance reach out to those who are willing step up and be a good Samaritan. The Help Now App gives those who need help post their location, and gives them the ability to reach out to those who are willing to offer aid. This isn't limited to natural disasters but is for almost any situation that ranges from being stuck in the snow or out of gas to your cat being stuck up a tree.
